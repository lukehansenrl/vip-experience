# Ad Angles — Starting Matrix

The angles to test in week 1. **Rank-specific frustration is the hero** — it's the one Luke has the strongest conviction will work, and we have a deep library of rank-specific thumbnails to pair with it.

Every angle ends with the same CTA destination: `/vip` → 15-sec VSL → 11-question application → Calendly. **No angle sells the offer in the ad itself.** The ad's only job is to magnetize the right click.

**Two absolute rules** for every hook below:
1. ❌ **Never mention the price** ($497) or any price proxy ("high-ticket", "premium", "$500-level"). See [product-brief.md](product-brief.md#price-secrecy-rule).
2. ✅ **Always pair with a thumbnail** from the SpookLuke library, not an AI-generated image. See [thumbnails-library.md](thumbnails-library.md).

## ⭐ Angle 1 (HERO): Rank-Specific Frustration

**Thesis:** Name the *specific* rank they're stuck at. The "Stuck in Diamond" ad outperforms the generic "Stuck in your rank" ad because the prospect feels seen. We split this into four variants — Plat, Diamond, Champ, GC — and serve each one to anyone-of-that-rank (let Meta's algorithm sort the targeting based on engagement signals).

### 1a. Stuck in Plat

**Hooks:**
- "Stuck in Plat for 18 months? You're not bad. You don't have a plan."
- "Plat 2 → Plat 3 → Plat 2 → Plat 3. Watch this if that's been your last 6 months."
- "Plat for 1,000 hours. Diamond for the next 100. Here's the difference."
- "Why most Plats never see Diamond. (It's not mechanics.)"
- "Hardstuck Plat. Here's what nobody tells you."

**Thumbnail candidates** (from the library):
- Anything with a Plat emblem + chains/lock motif
- "How to Position in Platinum" variants (Nov 2025)
- "Diamond in 30 Days" (2024) — aspirational rank-up visual

### 1b. Stuck in Diamond

**Hooks:**
- "Hard-stuck Diamond 2? It's not your aim. It's not your car control."
- "Watched 100 hours of Squishy and still hard-stuck Diamond?"
- "Diamond 3 → Champ 1. The gap nobody can teach you on YouTube."
- "1,500 hours and still Diamond. Read this."
- "Stuck in Diamond. Played for years. Sound familiar?"

**Thumbnail candidates:**
- Diamond emblem + lock/chain visuals
- "Diamond in 30 Days" — direct aspirational
- "Smurfing on Diamonds KBM" — proof-of-skill-gap visual
- Any "How to break Diamond" / "Diamond mistakes" themed thumb

### 1c. Stuck in Champ

**Hooks:**
- "Champ 1 ceiling? Watch this."
- "Champ 2 for a year. GC feels impossible. Here's why it isn't."
- "It's not your aim. It's not your car control. It's that nobody has ever actually watched your replays."
- "3,000 hours. Still Champ. Here's what's missing."
- "The Champ → GC wall is mental, not mechanical."

**Thumbnail candidates:**
- "Champ Hardstuck Plain" / "Champ Arrow Hardstuck" (2023) — these are perfect, literal "stuck Champ" visuals
- "This Is How You Reach GC" (2023) — direct progression visual
- Champ emblem + chains/lock

### 1d. Stuck just-below-GC

**Hooks:**
- "GC1 → GC2 → GC1. The MMR plateau every almost-GC hits."
- "You're not bad. You're just doing what got you to Champ. It won't get you to GC."

**Thumbnail candidates:**
- "Speedrun to GC" (2023)
- GC logo with lock/chain
- "This Is How You Reach GC"

**Note on targeting:** Don't try to manually segment ad sets by rank — let one ad set with all four variants run, and Meta will allocate spend to the variants that resonate (which will correlate with rank). The rank-specific copy *is* the targeting.

## Angle 2: You + Rank Logo + Timeline (Direct visual promise)

**Thesis:** Luke flagged a high-performing format: direct second-person + visual of the rank you want + a timeline. "You. Champ logo. 30 days." Pure pattern interrupt, instantly legible.

**Hook variants (very short — these are visual-led ads with minimal copy):**
- "You. Champ logo. 30 days."
- "You. Diamond. 6 weeks."
- "You. GC. This year."
- "You + this rank + a real plan."
- "Imagine your profile with this logo next to it."

**Visual format:**
- Take a thumbnail with a clean rank emblem (Champ, Diamond, GC) as the focal element
- Burn 2-3 words of copy on top: "YOU. CHAMP. 30 DAYS."
- Minimal text, maximum visual punch

**Thumbnail candidates:**
- Any clean rank-emblem thumbnail
- "Diamond in 30 Days" (2024) — already this format
- "Fastest Way To Rank Up" (2024)

**Best for:** Cold traffic, Reels/Stories placement (9:16). Scroll-stopper.

## Testing protocol

- **Week 1:** Launch Angle 1 (rank-specific frustration, all 4 variants) + Angle 2 ("You + rank") as the cold-traffic test. ~12-16 statics total. One CBO campaign, Lead objective. $50-100/day.
- **Day 3:** Kill anything with CTR < 0.8% or CPL > 2× target after 50+ impressions.
- **Week 2:** Promote winners. Add Angle 4 (Pro access) and Angle 3 (Guarantee) as the second batch. ~10 new ads.
- **Week 3:** Introduce video re-cuts from `existing-assets.md`.
- **Week 4:** Add retargeting layer with Angle 6 (social proof) and Angle 7 (scarcity). 1% Lookalike off Lead pixel.

**One change at a time.** Creative AND audience AND budget all changing in the same week = no learning.
